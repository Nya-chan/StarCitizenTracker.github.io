(Re)moving All Ships - 2018.10.03

As part of our ongoing optimization process, the StarCitizen Tracker team has decided to make some changes to the main page layout. These changes are intended to improve the readability of the main page and reduce extraneous information.

Whereas StarCitizen Tracker is primarily concerned with an accurate and concise overview of gameplay expectations, information regarding the completion of simple assets will be displayed less prominently starting today.

The first and primary change will be to move information regarding ships off the main page and onto starcitizentracker.github.io/ships. Other assets may follow in due course such as planetary systems. Information regarding ships and their playable status is often better served by several other great fansites in any case. starcitizen.tools/Category:Ships stands out in our mind.

With regards to these changes, careful consideration is being taken in cases where assets have specific features related to it. For example, if certain ships are closely linked to in-game features (such as "Data transportation" or "Refuelling") then those features will be listed on the main page individually so as not to affect the ease of use and completeness.

Contributing Factors

  • Overabundance of reports on ship status changes and new ships
  • Lack of community feedback and source links for gameplay elements
  • Many ships are official stretch goals which prevent removal entirely
